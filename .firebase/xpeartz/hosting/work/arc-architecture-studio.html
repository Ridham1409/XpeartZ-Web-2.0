The Challenge

Arc Studio's existing website did not reflect the calibre of their work. Potential clients were not converting because the portfolio felt dated and hard to navigate.

Our Approach

We built a custom Next.js website with a full-screen portfolio viewer, editorial typography, and a content strategy that positioned their work as art.

The Outcome

Organic enquiries increased by 180%. Three new major residential projects were won directly through the website within the first quarter.
